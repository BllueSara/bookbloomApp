# BookBloom

BookBloom is a dynamic application that connects writers and readers. It allows authors to create, publish, and protect their work in a secure and creative environment. Readers can explore stories, engage with authors, and support their creative endeavors.

## Introduction

BookBloom provides an innovative platform for authors to publish and protect their work while giving readers an engaging experience to discover unique content and directly interact with writers.

---

## Features

1. **User Authentication**
   - Securely sign up, log in, and manage user accounts.
   - Password recovery and profile customization.

2. **Write and Read Workspace**
   - Draft, edit, and publish stories.
   - Easily access published content.

3. **Book Lists**
   - Create and organize personal book lists.
   - Explore shared lists from other users.

4. **Interaction Features**
   - Like and comment on stories.
   - Provide feedback to authors.

5. **Content Protection**
   - Anti-copy features to safeguard intellectual property.
   - Mint works as NFTs to verify ownership.

---

## Usage

- Authors can draft and publish their work with built-in protections.
- Readers can explore stories, interact with authors, and organize their favorite content into lists.

---

## System Requirements

- **Operating Systems:** Android and iOS.
- **Integration:** The app uses Firebase for user management, data storage, and content protection.

---

## Examples

### Publishing a New Story
1. Log in to the app.
2. Navigate to the write workspace.
3. Start writing your story and click "Save" to save it as a draft.
4. Once ready, click "Publish" to make it available to readers.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue                           | Solution                                     |
|---------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Unable to log in                | Ensure you are entering the correct email and password. |
| Content not appearing after publishing | Check your network connection and try refreshing. |
| Likes or comments not saving    | Ensure the app is updated and your network is stable. |
